# 🚀 Digital Swarm Marketing & Ad Strategy

## 🎯 Target Audience

1. **Indie Hackers & Solo Entrepreneurs**: People looking to launch AI startups fast.
2. **Consultants & Agencies**: Looking for "Agents-as-a-Service" templates to sell to local businesses.
3. **Developers**: Looking for clean, ready-to-use Next.js + AI agent codebases.
4. **Crypto/Finance Enthusiasts**: Specifically for the *Swarm Finance Infiltrator*.

## 📢 Channels

### 1. Google Search Ads (High Intent)

- **Objective**: Capture users searching for "buy ai agent source code", "crewai boilerplate", "autonomous sales agent python".
- **Focus Products**: Sentinel Research Infiltrator, Swarm Sales Team.

### 2. Meta Ads (Instagram/Facebook)

- **Objective**: Brand awareness and retargeting using high-fidelity visuals (Holographic boxes, UI screenshots).
- **Targeting**: Interests in LangChain, OpenAI, Tech Startups, and Web Development.

### 3. Twitter/X Ads

- **Objective**: Direct engagement with the "build-in-public" community.
- **Creative**: Video walkthroughs of the agents in action.

- [ ] Install **Google Analytics 4** (GA4) Tracking.

- **Target ROAS**: 3.5x
- **Target CPA**: < ₹800 ($10)
