## 1. Research & competitive intel

**Competitor teardown (single company)**  
Act as a senior strategy consultant. Company: [NAME], domain: [URL], geography: [GEO]. Produce: (a) positioning vs top 3 alternatives, (b) pricing/packaging signals, (c) hiring & roadmap clues from public sources, (d) 5 attack vectors for a challenger, (e) 5 defences if we are the incumbent. Cite only public, verifiable facts; label inference clearly.

**Market map**  
Define the category [CATEGORY] in [GEO]. Segment players into: leaders, fast followers, niche, legacy. For each segment list 3 companies, typical ACV band, primary channel, and whitespace. End with 3 partnership ideas and 3 M&A-style consolidation plays (hypothetical).

**Trend surveillance brief**  
Scan the last 14 days of public discourse (newsletters, GitHub releases, HN, Reddit, X) for [NICHE]. Output: 5 signals with strength (low/med/high), 3 overhyped topics, 3 underpriced opportunities for a builder.
