Step 01 — Generate Your First JD

Extract swarm-talent-optimized.zip and open jd-templates/. Select your role type (Engineering, Sales, Marketing, Operations, etc.) and use the prompt in GPT-4 or Claude. Generate a compelling, bias-reduced job description in under 3 minutes — formatted for LinkedIn, Indeed, or your ATS.

Step 02 — Screen Resumes Instantly

Open screening-framework/. Build an AI scoring rubric for your specific role by inputting your required skills, experience level, and deal-breakers. Then paste any resume into the prompt to get a structured evaluation and shortlist recommendation in seconds.

Step 03 — Run Structured Interviews

Open interview-question-bank/. Select your role and seniority level to get a customized 8–10 question interview set covering technical competency, culture fit, and behavioral indicators. Each question includes scoring guidance and follow-up probes.

Step 04 — Automate LinkedIn Sourcing

Import the Make.com blueprint from sourcing-automation/. Configure your search parameters (role, location, company size, keywords) — the system automatically collects, deduplicates, and organizes LinkedIn candidates into a structured database for outreach.
