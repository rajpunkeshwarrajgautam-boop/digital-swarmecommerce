Deployment Protocol — 4 Steps

Step 01 — Read Compliance Notes First

Before using any patient-facing prompts, open compliance-notes.md. This outlines compliant AI usage in clinical settings and identifies which prompt types require licensed practitioner review before delivery.

⚕️ Clinical Use Notice: Always have a licensed practitioner review AI-generated clinical content (SOAP notes, referral letters, discharge summaries) before finalizing. These prompts are drafting and research aids — not diagnostic tools or medical advice.

Step 02 — Extract & Start Clinical Documentation

Extract swarm-voice.zip. Open Section 2 of prompt-library.md for SOAP note drafts, referral letters, and discharge summaries. Paste your clinical notes into ChatGPT-4 or Claude and get a structured draft immediately.

Step 03 — Set Up Patient Communications

Open patient-comms-templates/ for appointment reminders, follow-up emails, and patient FAQ responses. All templates are pre-formatted for email or SMS delivery — customize with your practice name and physician details.

Step 04 — Automate Scheduling & Intake

Import blueprints from workflow-blueprints/ into Make.com to automate appointment confirmation workflows and new patient intake questionnaires — reducing admin time by 2+ hours per day.
