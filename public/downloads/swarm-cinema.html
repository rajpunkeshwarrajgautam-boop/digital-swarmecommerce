Deployment Protocol — 4 Steps

Step 01 — Generate Your First Script

Extract swarm-cinema.zip and open script-engine/. Choose your format: short-form (15–60s), YouTube long-form, documentary, or ad spot. Use the multi-step prompt chain in GPT-4 or Claude — input your concept, get a production-ready script structure with hooks, scenes, and CTAs.

Step 02 — Create Your Shot List

Open shot-list-generator.md. Paste your script (or scene summary) — the AI generates a detailed shot-by-shot production list with camera angles, movement directions, and recommended timing. Formats: horizontal, vertical (Reels/TikTok), and widescreen cinema.

Step 03 — Automate Distribution

Import blueprints from distribution-automation/ into Make.com. Once a video uploads to your Google Drive or Dropbox trigger folder, the system auto-posts to YouTube, Instagram Reels, and TikTok with AI-generated platform-optimized captions simultaneously.

Step 04 — Optimize for SEO & Discovery

Open seo-optimizer.md. Paste your video concept or script summary — get YouTube title variants (A/B tested), description with keyword clusters, chapter markers, and TikTok/Instagram hashtag sets. All generated for maximum algorithmic reach.
