Quick Start — 3 Steps to Deploy

Step 01 — Log In to Notion

Go to notion.so and sign in to your account. If you don't have one, create a free account — it takes 60 seconds and no credit card is required.

Step 02 — Duplicate Your CRM Blueprint

Click the button below to open your private CRM template. Once the Notion page loads, click the Duplicate button in the top-right corner to instantly copy the entire system into your workspace.

Access Your CRM Blueprint →

Opens in Notion. Click "Duplicate" to install into your workspace.

Step 03 — Configure Your Hub

Inside your new CRM, open the ⚙️ Settings page (linked in the sidebar). Update your hourly rate, default currency, and brand name. Add your first client to the Clients database — the revenue dashboard auto-calculates everything from there.

Step 04 — Add Your First Project

Navigate to the Projects board. Create a new project, link it to your client, set a deadline and budget. Your pipeline, timeline, and invoice tracker will populate automatically.
