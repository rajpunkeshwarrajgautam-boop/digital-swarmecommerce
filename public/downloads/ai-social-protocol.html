Protocol: AI Social Poster Automation

You will need a free Make.com account and an OpenAI API key to fuel the intelligence engine.

Step 02: Import the Blueprint

Download the secure `.json` blueprint file below. Then, go to your Make.com dashboard, create a new scenario, click "More" at the bottom screen, and select "Import Blueprint".
